About

Making the world a little bit more beautiful one bouquet at a time.

My name is Karim Preuss and Dandelion is my pride. We’re a flower boutique located in the heart of Alameda’s Webster St. We know how important it is to have a reliable community partner who’s there for all of your biggest moments, which is why we work directly with local growers to bring our customers the freshest blooms available. Dandelion has grown from a humble flower stand that I started in 2010 to a full-service floral resource, offering elegant arrangements and full scale event service. We are a proud woman-owned local small business, and are so privileged to be a part of a community of local artisans and craftsmen.
